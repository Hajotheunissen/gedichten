This is whisky poetry. It stems from the German tradition of dichten (to compact) which means that big stories, are being compacted into small lines of 'truth'. These are not to be read in large numbers at the same time. These are to be taken in like a good glass of whisky, bourbon if you like.

Have one, have another and then let it sip into consciousness. Slowly.

Enjoy :)

Hajo J. Theunissen.
